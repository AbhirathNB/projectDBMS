Question : What are the minimum system requirements I need for online exams?

Answer : You will need a working webcam (preferably external) and microphone, and a stable internet connection. You should take your written examination on either a desktop computer or laptop

Question : I'm an overseas candidate. Will I have to take my examination at an unusual time?

Answer : Examinations will be held at the same time across the world in line with GMT so if you are based overseas then your examination will take place at the equivalent time.

Question : How will you stop candidates from cheating?

Answer : The Examomatic system will lock down the device that you are using so that the only thing you can access is your examination.

Question : What happens if I am late for the examination?

Answer : For written examinations, if you are more than 30 minutes late you will not be able to sit.

Question : What internet speed do I need?

Answer : The Examomatic team has advised that a minimum of 516kbps connection speed is recommended.
